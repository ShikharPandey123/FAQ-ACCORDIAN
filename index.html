FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me ?

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML,CSS and Javascript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free ?

There are five difficulty levels to the challenges that they offer, and most of the challenges are free. However, there are some premium challenges that require a monthly or yearly fee to access.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

You can sharpen your skills building websites and add finished projects to your portfolio to help land a job! ... Add projects to your learning journey and put your knowledge to the test. The real learning happens when you start solving real-world problems yourself.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?

The best (and quickest) way to get help on a challenge is in our Discord server. There are thousands of other developers in there, so it's a great place to ask questions.
